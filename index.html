blacksmithing

    What is blacksmithing

    Blacksmiths work with different types of metal to create different kinds of tools and objects. Using a forge to heat and soften the metal, blacksmiths then use an anvil and a hammer to hammer the material into the appropriate shapes.

The average Blacksmith salary in the United States is $43,407 as of March 26, 2024, but the salary range typically falls between $34,474 and $62,956.
